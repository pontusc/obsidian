## Ingredients
- 6 chicken quarters, skin on
- 450g small potatoes, halved
- 1 large onion
- 4 cloves garlic
- 15ml salt
- 7ml black pepper
- 20ml oregano, plus extra for sprinkling
- 120ml lemon juice
- 60ml Dijon mustard
- 45ml water
- 45ml olive oil

## Steps
**Step 1**
Add onion, garlic, salt, black pepper, oregano, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, water and olive oil to a blender. Blend until smooth.

**Step 2**
Pour marinade over chicken quarters in a deep pan. Using hands, coat both sides thoroughly. Arrange chicken and potatoes in the pan. Sprinkle additional oregano over the top.

**Step 3**
Line pan with parchment paper. Bake at 200Â°C for 50 to 60 minutes until chicken is cooked through and golden.
